**Verdict paragraph:**

> On the complex PasarKerja Migran draft, five outputs were scored. Claude and BIF repeatedly land at or near 25 with idea-specific hinges around unlicensed/gray platform economics plus thin crisis infrastructure; GLM is frequently co-strong. ChatGPT and Gemini more often tell a standard regulatory-kill story. BIF’s distinctive angle—partner agencies incentivized to abandon the license shield, plus debt-bondage media framing and full product packaging—keeps it in the top band. One strict re-score put Claude alone at 25 and BIF at 22, so the honest read is **co-lead / setara with Claude**, not universal wipeout. Quality delta vs weaker free-form is real; vs Claude-class on thick regulated marketplaces, expect a photo finish.

**Next product actions (for the *idea* founder — not BIF eng):**

1. Crisis infra before scale (hotline, housing fund, insurance).  
2. Clear licensing / JV — stop pure “tech facade” if capturing recruiter economics.  
3. Partner exit clauses + rail redundancy (fintech/lender/agency).

**Next for BIF product:**

1. Treat this as evidence BIF **holds on complex multi-constraint ideas**.  
2. Steal Claude’s communication of “profit vs accountability” as a framing pattern in prompts if still undershot.  
3. Keep one-spine pressure — punish cascade bloat (remittance + app store + hawala as parallel plots).
